SAMARBHUMI — War Never Ends (v1.2 Multiplayer Update)
==================================================

1. PRE-INSTALLATION INFORMATION
-------------------------------
SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS:
- OS: Windows 10/11, macOS, or Linux (64-bit)
- Java: JDK 17 or later (The installer will bundle this)
- Network: Stable internet connection (required for Online Multiplayer)
- Display: 1280x720 recommended

PLAYER 1 CONTROLS:
- Move: A / D
- Jump: W (Double-tap for Double Jump)
- Crouch: S
- Jetpack: E (Hold)
- Fire: Left Mouse Button (Mouse Aim)
- Melee: Q
- Reload: R
- Swap Weapon: Tab

PLAYER 2 CONTROLS:
- Move: Arrow Keys (Left/Right)
- Jump: Up Arrow (Double-tap for Double Jump)
- Crouch: Down Arrow
- Jetpack: Numpad 8
- Fire: Numpad 0
- Melee: Numpad 5
- Reload: Numpad 5

Press ESC during gameplay to Pause.
